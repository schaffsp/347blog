This week with midterm exams I decided to do something interesting that wouldn't nessecarily challege my programing skills. I wanted to embedd audio into future projects, and after looking at the different ways to play audio I decided on the following as not only the best but also one of the easiest.

Hopefully, the above allows you to play, pause, adjust volume, and adjust playback speed of a concerned citizen addressing President Joeseph R. Biden. If you see the message "Your browser does no support the audio element", I tried Chrome and Firefox with no issue.

<audio controls>

This acts as a container for whatever audio you intend to play. You can put multiple different files of different types, such ass ogg, mp3, and etc. Right now I only have one:

 <source src="Joe_Biden_Wake_Up.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">

Now all thats left to do is close it like so.

</audio>
